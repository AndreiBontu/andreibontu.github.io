In summary, what I built is:

  • the possibility of uploading one or multiple images.

  • the possibility to follow or unfollow a user.
  • the notification system, in case if a user liked or commented one of your post.
  • the possibility to watch the posts of your followings on your newsfeed.
  • other little functionalities
