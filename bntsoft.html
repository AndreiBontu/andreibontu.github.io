BNT SOFT

BNT SOFT is a simple and easy to use application that helps you quickly create your entry-reception notes.
BNT SOFT automatically calculates the entry notes with trade markup, being sufficient to enter only the name of the goods, the purchase price without VAT and the sale price, the rest of the fields being calculated and completed by the soft. BNT SOFT memorizes product names as well as purchase and sale prices so that at the moment of a new supply, it's sufficient to select the product from the list.
Moreover, bnt soft keep a record for all the entry-reception notes have you made as well the invoices. The possibility to print each entry-reception notes after you have complete it.
